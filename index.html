Perfect Shapes Support

Welcome to the official support page for Perfect Shapes, the ultimate shape-drawing game!

Version 2.0.0

Perfect Shapes is a fun and challenging game where you can practice drawing perfect shapes in single mode or compete with up to 10 friends in multiplayer mode.

New Features in Version 2.0.0

  • New Game Modes: Trace Mode and Free Draw Mode (with the addition of a Straight Line tool).
  • Multiplayer Mode: Now play with up to 10 friends in 5 rounds.
  • Available Shapes: Circle, Square, and Triangle.

Current Features

  • Play Mode: Practice drawing perfect shapes in single mode.
  • Multiplayer Mode: Play with up to 10 friends and compete in up to 5 rounds.
  • Currently Available Shapes: Circle, Square, and Triangle.
